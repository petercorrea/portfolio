bio

Based in Miami, Peter is a full-stack engineer with an education from Florida International University. He enjoys software engineering because it sits on the intersection of abstract thinking, logical comprehension, and creative design. His stack normally includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, NodeJS, Express, GraphQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Vercel and Heroku. Current projects include Centr: the minimal social app, Strides: a companion for better habits, weGather: meetups for everyone, myHalf: the dating app for real connections, and GiveMeTheDeets: a demographical research tool for discovering your next city.

Previous experience includes leading big-box retail teams where he managed operations, online fulfillment, and drove financial profitability. He brings out the best of any team by encouraging learning on the fly and failing fast while staying laser focused on the vision and the end-user. His philosophy involves approachability, understanding others, values, and customer focus. His strenghths include dealing with ambiguity and strategic agility. As an autodidact, Peter values in investing in self-development and personal learning. In his spare time he's learning more about his favorite topics: psychology, engineering, design, typography, minimalism, cryptography, and discrete mathematics. When not in front of a computer you can find him meditating, practicing photography, and mentoring others to code.
